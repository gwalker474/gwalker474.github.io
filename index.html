About

I am a photographer living in South Yorkshire, UK. I have a particular passion for photographing sports and especially recreational or 'club'-level sport. From many years' involvement as a spectator, a player, and a parent, I know that many people put their heart and soul into recreational sport, both on and off the fields of play. I love capturing that skill, effort, emotion, energy—that life—and showing it to the world through my photography. If you think I can help you or your club capture and share your story through photography please get in touch with me and we can start a conversation about how we can make that happen.

Before turning to photography full-time I worked in higher education for more than 20 years as a teacher and researcher. (I graduated with a doctorate/PhD in 2004.) I love learning how to do things and sharing my skills with others. I always strive to improve and achieve new things with my photography. So when you work with me, you know you are working with someone who wants to reach new heights and who will help you to do the same.
